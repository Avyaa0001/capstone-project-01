Traditional Fashion of Chhattisgarh

The Kosa Silk Kurta

is a luxurious traditional garment worn by men in Chhattisgarh, made from the famous Kosa silk. Known for its natural golden-brown hue and fine texture, this fabric is handwoven by skilled artisans, particularly from the Champa and Korba regions. The Kurta is usually paired with a Dhoti or Pichhori and is worn during special occasions such as weddings, religious festivals, and cultural gatherings. The Kosa Silk Kurta represents elegance and wealth, as Kosa silk is considered a prestigious fabric in India. It has cultural importance in the state and is often passed down as a family heirloom.
