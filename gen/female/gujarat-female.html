Traditional Fashion of Gujarat

Chaniya-Choli

The women in the image are wearing traditional Gujarati clothing, which is often brightly colored and decorated with intricate embroidery. The most common type of Gujarati women's clothing is the chaniya choli, which consists of a short, fitted blouse and a long, flowing skirt. The women in the image are also wearing ghaghra cholis, which are a type of chaniya choli with a flared skirt.
