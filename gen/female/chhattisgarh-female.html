Traditional Fashion of Chhattisgarh

The Lugada :

Is a traditional dress of Chhattisgarh, not Madhya Pradesh. It is a type of saree that is typically made of cotton and is worn with a blouse called a Polkha. The Lugada is often decorated with embroidery or other embellishments. It is a popular choice for women in Chhattisgarh, especially for special occasions. Here are some of the reasons why the Lugada is considered a traditional dress of Chhattisgarh: * It is a long-standing tradition in Chhattisgarh for women to wear the Lugada. * The Lugada is made from materials that are readily available in Chhattisgarh. Here are the specifications of the Lugada: * Material: Cotton * Colors: Typically red, maroon, or dark blue * Design: Embroidered with geometric patterns * Style: Worn with a Kachora (saree style) and a Polkha (blouse) The Lugada is a beautiful and practical garment that is perfect for the hot and humid climate of Chhattisgarh.
